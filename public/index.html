How long does it take to implement?

48 hours from signup to going live. Day 1: setup call and configuration. Day 2: quick staff training (5 min/person) and launch. We guarantee it.

Do my staff need to download an app?

No! Staff use WhatsApp, which they already have on their phones. They just receive task notifications and tap YES or NO. That's it. No new apps, no training headaches.

What if my staff don't speak English?

Efyoos supports English, French, Arabic, and we're adding more languages monthly. Staff see messages in their preferred language automatically.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256), host on secure servers, and comply with GDPR. Your guest data and operations are protected with enterprise-grade security.

Can I try it before committing?

Absolutely. We offer a 14-day free trial with full features and support. No credit card required. See if Efyoos works for your hotel with zero risk.

What happens if I need help?

Email support responds within 4 hours. Early adopters get priority support with 1-hour response time. Plus, we proactively monitor your system during the first month.

Will this work for a small hotel?

Yes! Efyoos works for hotels of any size - from 10 rooms to 500+ rooms. The system scales with you. Small hotels often see the biggest impact because efficiency gains are immediate.

Yes, no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. We're confident you'll love Efyoos, but we don't believe in locking customers in.
